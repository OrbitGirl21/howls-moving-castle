When an unconfident young woman is cursed with an old body by a spiteful witch, her only chance of breaking the spell lies with a self-indulgent yet insecure young wizard and his companions in his legged, walking castle. In this film she learns about embracing the unbecomings that happen to her, and she learns to fall in love, and accept herself for who she is.
